Our Story

MADE BY SOMEONE WHO WEARS THE BADGE

MicroPatches was founded by an active duty law enforcement officer and United States Army National Guard veteran based in Phoenix, Arizona. We specialize in UV-printed 3D scaled-down patch keychains, magnets, and croc charms for law enforcement, military, fire, EMS, and custom designs. Everything we make is USA made with no minimum orders so no agency or unit gets left out. Our long term mission is to donate a portion of all proceeds to the families of fallen officers, veterans, and first responders.
