Our Mission

We are on a mission to 3D print a miniature patch keychain for every law enforcement agency, fire department, military unit, and first responder organization in the world. Every single one. Completely free. You just pay the cost of the keychain.

Our long term goal is to donate a portion of all proceeds to the families of fallen officers, veterans, and first responders.
