Questions

Good to know

Need a brand new design?

Absolutely. We create original work from scratch — memorial patches, retirement pieces, morale designs, or fresh artwork. Pricing is handled case by case; email officialmicropatches@gmail.com or note it in your request.

Bulk order discounts?

Yes. The $25 design fee can be waived on qualifying bulk orders, and unit pricing improves with volume. Tell us your estimated quantity and we'll send a tailored quote.

What photo works best?

Lay the patch flat on a solid surface and shoot straight down from directly overhead with even lighting and no shadows. A vector or high-resolution digital file is ideal if you have one.

How long does production take?

It depends on quantity and design complexity. We confirm a firm timeline with your quote, typically after the design is approved.

Can you make PVC-style patches?

No — we don't produce embroidered patches or PVC. That said, our Flexible Patches are similar to PVC, with immaculate detail and durability. The rest of our line is 3D-printed and finished with premium UV-cured ink for a raised, textured feel, and we can recreate almost any patch design in that format.

Can I submit multiple patch designs?

Yes. Submit one request and list each design in the notes, or email the rest. Each distinct design carries its own $25 setup fee, which can be waived on qualifying bulk orders.
