§ ABOUT · PHOENIX, AZ

Carry your patch.
Anywhere.

MicroPatches is active law enforcement owned, based in Phoenix, Arizona. We make premium miniature patch products inspired by the patches worn on duty — for law enforcement, fire, EMS, military, and public safety.

§ Our Story

Made by someone
who wears the badge.

MicroPatches was founded by an active-duty law enforcement officer in Phoenix, Arizona. The idea was simple: take the patch you wear on your uniform — the one that means something to you, your unit, your department — and scale it down so you can carry it on your keys, your bag, or your lapel.

Every MicroKeychain is built in Phoenix — a 3D-printed base finished with premium UV-cured ink for the design. We use the same care for an order of one as we do for an order of fifty. That's why we don't enforce minimums on custom orders — no agency, no unit, no department gets left out.

Our long-term mission is to give back to the families of fallen officers, veterans, and first responders. Every piece we make is a small reminder of the people who choose to serve.
