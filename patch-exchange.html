Patch Exchange Program

THE PATCH EXCHANGE PROGRAM

FREE KEYCHAIN JUST PAY FOR SHIPPING

Send us your patch and we will transform it into a custom Micro Keychain inspired by your original design. Every patch submitted becomes part of the growing Micro Patches collection.

HOW IT WORKS

1

MAIL YOUR PATCH

Mail your patch to:

Micro Patches
PO Box 123
Phoenix, AZ 85044

Include your name, email, return mailing address, and any notes inside the envelope.

2

WE CREATE YOUR MICRO KEYCHAIN

Once we receive your patch, we photograph it, complete the design and workup process, and produce your custom Micro Keychain.

3

YOUR KEYCHAIN SHIPS BACK

You pay to ship your patch to us. We pay to ship your completed Micro Keychain back to you.

Submission Guidelines

WHAT WE ACCEPT

We accept any EMBROIDERED or PVC STYLE patch for LAW ENFORCEMENT, FIRE DEPARTMENTS, EMS, and MILITARY.

Not one of these and still think we might want the patch? Send us an email at OfficialMicroPatches@gmail.com

PATCH OWNERSHIP NOTICE

By sending us your patch, you agree that Micro Patches may keep the original patch and add it to our permanent collection. In return, you receive one custom Micro Keychain based on your submitted patch.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Will I get my original patch back?

No. By participating in the Patch Exchange Program, you agree that Micro Patches may keep the original patch and add it to our permanent collection. In exchange, you receive one custom Micro Keychain based on your submitted patch.

Who pays for shipping?

You pay to ship your patch to us. We pay to ship your completed Micro Keychain back to you.

What type of patches do you accept?

We accept embroidered and PVC style patches for law enforcement, fire departments, EMS, and military.

How long does it take?

Turnaround time varies depending on current order volume and design complexity. Rush processing may be available when production capacity allows.

Can I order additional keychains?

Yes. Additional Micro Keychains can be requested with your exchange order.
