Patch Exchange Program

THE PATCH EXCHANGE PROGRAM

Send us your patch. We return it plus a precision UV-printed keychain version.

HOW IT WORKS

1

MAIL YOUR PATCH

Mail your patch to: 14814 S 44th Pl, Phoenix, AZ 85044

Include your name, email, and return address inside the envelope.

2

WE BUILD YOUR KEYCHAIN

We photograph, scan, and UV print to match your patch exactly. Every detail preserved.

3

EVERYTHING SHIPS BACK

Your original patch ships back with your new keychain. Turnaround is 1 to 2 weeks from receipt.

What We Accept

  • Any embroidered or woven patch
  • Department patches and unit crests
  • Memorial patches
  • Challenge coin designs
  • Retirement and commemorative patches

We treat every patch with care and return all patches.

Exchange patches are processed in the standard queue in the order they arrive. Once we get to your patch and produce it, you receive one free keychain or magnet. Or apply it as a discount toward a custom order instead. You just cover shipping. No timeline guarantees but we will take care of you.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Will I get my original patch back?

Yes. Always. We return every patch we receive. Your original ships back with your new keychain.

How long does it take?

Standard turnaround is 1 to 2 weeks from the time we receive your patch. Rush processing gets it done within 5 business days for an extra $5.

What if I want more than one keychain?

Each additional unit is $12.99. Just let us know in the request form how many you want.

Can I apply the exchange toward a custom order instead?

Yes. You can use your free keychain credit toward a custom order with a design fee instead. Just note that in the request form.
